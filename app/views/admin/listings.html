Induction tutor/school management

All schools on sign in land at the Manage your training page, which has different functionality depending on induction programme chosen below.

Core induction programme (CIP)

When a school is delivering their own using DfE accredited materials. User journey on Miro

  • Manage your training
    • Do you know which accredited materials you want to use?
    • Which training materials do you want this cohort to use?
    • Training materials confirmed
    • Your accredited materials
    • Choose your induction programme

Full induction programme (FIP)

When a school is using a training provider funded by DfE.
User journey on Miro

  • Manage your training
    • Signing up with a training provider
    • Choose your induction programme
    • Report that your school has been signed up incorrectly
    • Your report has been submitted

    Notified via email of a new partnership. User journey on Miro

  • Provider confirmed (email)

Deliver your own (DYO)

When a school designs and deliver their own programme based on the Early Career Framework (ECF).

  • Manage your training
    • Choose your induction programme

Not expecting any ECT (noECT)

When a school is not expecting to have any ECTs but has an induction tutor.

  • Manage your training
