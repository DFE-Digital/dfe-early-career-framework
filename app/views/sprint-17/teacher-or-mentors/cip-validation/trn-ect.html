A teacher reference number (TRN) is a unique ID that helps us to check you’re a teacher.

If you do not know your teacher reference number (TRN), you can usually find it on your payslip, teachers’ pension documents, or teacher training records.

QTS, GTC, DfE, DfES or DCSF numbers are the same as TRNs.

You may not have a TRN if you qualified outside England.
