From: Department for Education: Teacher Continuing Professional Development

Subject: Sign in to manage training for early career teachers

Hello {{ data.sitName }},

{{ data.schoolName }} has nominated you to be their induction tutor and manage training for early career teachers (ECTs).

Read our step-by-step guidance to find out how your programme should be set up: https://manage-training-for-early-career-teachers.education.gov.uk/how-to-set-up-your-programme

When and why to use this DfE service

We need information from you to arrange relevant funding and access to training materials.

This is in addition to any information a training provider or appropriate body may ask you for.

Use our service to:

  • tell us if you expect any ECTs to join your school in the term ahead
  • choose or change how your school will run your ECT training programme
  • nominate someone else to be the induction tutor for your school instead

When to register your ECTs and mentors

If your school is using a DfE-funded training provider or accredited materials, you need to register your ECTs and their mentors with us directly.

An ECT will only become eligible for funded training once they receive their qualified teacher status (QTS).

You must use our service to tell us:

  • the name and email address of each new ECT
  • the name and email address of each mentor assigned to work with an ECT

How to sign in and use our service

  • Follow this link: https://manage-training-for-early-career-teachers.education.gov.uk/
  • Sign in using the email address we sent this message to. Be careful to enter your address correctly with no spaces. You do not need a password.
  • If you do not receive an email from the DfE when you try to sign in, check your spam or junk folder.

If you have any questions, reply to this email.
