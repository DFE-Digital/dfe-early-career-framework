{% elif (data['training-programme'] === "DYO") %}

What to do next

You need to:

  • design a 2-year programme of support and training that covers every ‘learn that’ and ‘learn how to’ statement in the Early Career Framework (ECF) (opens in new tab)
  • contact your appropriate body to find out what evidence is needed to demonstrate that your programme:
    • is based on the ECF
    • meets the statutory requirements

You do not need to add information about your ECTs and mentors to this service.

See our statutory guidance (open in new tab) for information about funding, time off, and roles.
