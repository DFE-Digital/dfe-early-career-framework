This service is for:

  • early career teachers (ECTs) working, or due to start work, in England
  • mentors assigned to work with ECTs during their induction training

We need to check your details in the Teaching Regulation Agency records. This is to make sure you’re eligible for this funded programme.

We’ll ask for your:

  • teacher reference number (TRN)
  • date of birth

If you have a TRN but cannot find it

You can enter your National Insurance number instead. This will help us find and check your TRN.

If you’re a mentor without a TRN

You must apply for a TRN before you can use this service. You need a TRN to receive funded training and materials.
