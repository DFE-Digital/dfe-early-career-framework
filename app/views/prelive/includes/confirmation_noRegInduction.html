Eligibility for funding

Your appropriate body must register {{ participant.name }}’s induction with the Teaching Regulation Agency (TRA).

This is an important step to make sure funding is in place for their statutory induction and ECF-based training.

Contact your appropriate body directly to arrange this.

  • ECF-based training
  • mentoring
  • time off timetable to take part in training and mentoring sessions
