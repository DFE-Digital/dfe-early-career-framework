What happens next

You’re now registered for DfE-funded mentor training to support ECTs.

We’ll pass your information to your school’s training provider. They’ll contact you directly with more information about your programme.

You should receive:

  • ECF-based mentor training
  • adequate time to take part in training

Where to find help and information

If you have any questions about how ECF-based training and mentoring works, ask your school’s induction tutor for guidance.

You can also read our guidance for mentors (opens in new tab).
