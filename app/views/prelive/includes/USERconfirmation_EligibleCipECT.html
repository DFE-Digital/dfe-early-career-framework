What happens next

You’re now registered for DfE-funded training and support during your 2-year early career teacher induction.

You’ll receive a link to your training materials within the next 24 hours. If you do not receive an email from us, check your spam or junk folder.

You should receive:

  • ECF-based training
  • mentoring
  • time off timetable to take part in training and mentoring sessions

Where to find help and information

If you have any questions about how your induction or ECF-based training works, ask your school’s induction tutor for guidance.

You can also read our guidance for ECTs (link opens in a new tab).
