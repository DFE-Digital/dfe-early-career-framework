How you can sign up with a training provider

There are a few ways you can sign up with a training provider:

The easiest way to sign up with a training provider is to contact your local Teaching School Hub (TSH). They can talk you through how to access this support for your early career teachers and mentors.

You can also explore what our 6 providers have to offer and contact them directly:

  • Ambition Institute
  • Best Practice Network (home of Outstanding Leaders Partnership)
  • Capita with lead academic partner the University of Birmingham
  • Education Development Trust
  • Teach First
  • UCL Institute of Education

These training providers and their local partners will also be recruiting schools in the next few months, so you may hear from them.
