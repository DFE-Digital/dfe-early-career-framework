Are you expecting any early career teachers (also known as NQTs) to join you in the 2021 to 2022 academic year?

Update the DfE here:

https://manage-training-for-early-career-teachers.education.gov.uk/nominations/start?token=22345678as9fijhgt

You only need to answer ‘yes’ or ‘no’.

What happens next

If you choose ‘yes’, we’ll guide you to set up your 2-year training programme, following the new statutory requirements.

If you choose ‘no’, we will not send you any more reminders until the next academic year.

Where to get more help and support

https://www.gov.uk/guidance/teaching-school-hubs
