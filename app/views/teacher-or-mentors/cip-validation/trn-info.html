If you do not know your teacher reference number (TRN), you can usually find it on your payslip, teachers' pension documents, or teacher training records.

QTS, GTC, DfE, DfES or DCSF numbers are the same as TRNs.

Your number:

  • is usually 7 digits, for example '4567814'
  • may include the letters 'RP' or a slash '/'' symbol, for example 'RP99/12345'

Request a TRN reminder email

Email qts.enquiries@education.gov.uk. You must include your:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number

Request a TRN reminder by phone

Call the Teaching Regulation Agency on 020 7593 5394.

Make sure you have your National Insurance number ready before you call.

Phone lines are open 9 AM to 5PM Monday to Thursday, and 9AM to 4:30 PM on Friday. Lines are closed on bank holidays.
