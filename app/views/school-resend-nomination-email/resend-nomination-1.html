You need to nominate an ‘induction tutor’ to set up your induction programme for early career teachers.

This is someone who can:

  • confirm details about your induction programme
  • add information about your early career teachers and mentors

Schools are sent a unique link to nominate someone but you can send a new link if this has expired or if the email is lost.

How does this work?

  • you enter a few details to find your school
  • we will send a nomination link to your school's email address on our register, Get Information about Schools (GIAS)
  • it will be an administration email, for example, office@, admin@ or head@
  • if you’re not sure, sign in to DfE and check that your GIAS email address is up to date
