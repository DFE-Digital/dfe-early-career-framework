From September 2021, new teachers in England must complete a 2-year induction after they have done their initial teacher training (ITT) or PGCE.

The induction must be based on the early career framework.

We have shared guidance with your school about:

  • the statutory changes to induction
  • the types of induction you can choose from
  • what our funded options have to offer

Now we invite you to register your school and confirm:

  • what type of induction you want to run
  • which early career teachers are starting a programme this year

Register by 30 September 2021 to start your induction programme.
