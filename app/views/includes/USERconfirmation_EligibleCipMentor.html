What happens next

You’ll receive a link to your training materials within the next 24 hours. If you do not receive an email from us, check your spam or junk folder.

Your school should give you adequate time to conduct mentoring sessions with ECTs.

Where to find help and information

If you have any questions about how ECF-based training and mentoring works, ask your school’s induction tutor for guidance.

You can also read our guidance for mentors (opens in new tab).
