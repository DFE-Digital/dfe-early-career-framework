What happens next

{% if data['inductionProgramme'] == "FIP" %}

We’ll pass your information to your school’s training provider. This means you’ll be ready to start once we confirm your eligibility.

They’ll contact you directly with more information about your programme.

Once we’ve confirmed you’re eligible, you should receive:

  • ECF-based mentor training
  • adequate time to take part in training
{% elif data['inductionProgramme'] == "CIP" %}

You’ll receive a link to your training materials within the next 24 hours. This means you’ll be ready to start once we confirm your eligibility.

If you do not receive anything, you should check you spam or junk folder.

If eligible, you should be given adequate time to conduct mentoring sessions with ECTs.
