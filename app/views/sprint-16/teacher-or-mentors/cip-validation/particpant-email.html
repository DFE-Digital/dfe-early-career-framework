Hello, [First name]

[school_name] has let us know that you’ll be joining them in 2021.

About your early career teacher training programme

All early career teachers now do a 2-year programme of high quality professional development and mentoring.

These programmes are underpinned by the Early Career Framework (ECF). This sets out what all early career teachers should learn about, and learn how to do, during the first 2 years of their careers.

Find out more by reading this guidance: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/early-career-framework

Get set up to start your programme

Before you can start, we need to check your details on the Teaching Regulation Agency records to make sure you qualify for this training and support.

We need to ask you for:

  • your teacher reference number (TRN).
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number (optional)
