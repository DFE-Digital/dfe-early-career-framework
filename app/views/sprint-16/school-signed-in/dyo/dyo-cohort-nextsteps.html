You have chosen to: design and deliver your own programme based on the early career framework (ECF)

What you can do now

The early career framework (ECF) covers 8 standards. For every standard there are statements for early career teachers to “learn that” and “learn how to”.

You need to:

  • design training that covers all of these statements
  • ask your appropriate body what evidence you will need to demonstrate that your programme meets the statutory requirements

What you need to do before your induction starts

  • make sure your early career teacher has enough time off in their timetable to complete training
  • make sure that your induction tutor and mentor have the time to carry out their roles

See our guidance for details on time off, roles and responsibilities

How to change your programme

If your school decides to:

  • use a training provider, funded by the DfE (full induction programme)

Or

  • deliver your own programme using DfE accredited materials (core induction programme)

Contact: continuing-professional-development@digital.education.gov.uk
