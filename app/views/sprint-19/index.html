  • how you want to run your training
  • which accredited materials or training provider you will use
  • which ECTs and mentors will be on the programme

You can use our step by step guide to help you complete these steps.

Your school must complete these steps before your early career teachers (ECTs) start their statutory induction programme.

For example, if your ECTs are starting in September, you need to complete these steps before then.
